Privacy Policy

Project Baseline Publisher — Effective March 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Project Baseline Publisher ("the App") is a TikTok application operated by Project Baseline, Inc., a Colorado Profit Corporation located in Broadview, IL. The App enables authorized users to publish branded photo content to TikTok accounts owned by the company.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with the App.

2. Information We Collect

When you authenticate with the App through TikTok, we collect the following information:

  • TikTok display name and avatar/profile picture from your TikTok account
  • TikTok user ID used to associate your account with the App
  • Authentication tokens required to publish content on your behalf

We do not collect passwords, payment information, private messages, follower lists, or any content from your TikTok account beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account with the App
  • Display your profile information within the admin panel
  • Publish photo posts to your TikTok account using TikTok's Content Posting API

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on a secured Virtual Private Server (VPS) with restricted access. We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is used exclusively for the purposes described in this policy.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your TikTok account is connected to the App. You may disconnect your account at any time through the admin panel, which will remove your stored authentication tokens and profile information from our systems.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your TikTok account at any time via the admin panel
  • Revoke the App's access through your TikTok account settings

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
