Common Questions

I've heard AI is mostly hype. How do I know this will actually work?

Fair question. Most AI conversation is hype. What we build is different because we use the same systems in our own operations every day. The 110+ agents in our ecosystem aren't demos or prototypes. They run production workflows across six ventures, handling real tasks with real consequences. We only recommend what we've already proven works.

How long does a typical engagement take?

The AI Readiness Self-Audit is generated within 5 minutes of submission. The Intelligence Report is delivered within 5 business days of intake. Brand Architecture Sprint is a two-week engagement. Workflow Automation projects typically run 1 to 8 weeks depending on scope. AI Agent Development at the Basic tier delivers in 3 weeks; larger multi-agent builds run 6 to 12 weeks. Political Campaign AI builds vary based on election timelines but can deploy initial systems in as little as 3 weeks. Fractional CAIO engagements are ongoing, with a minimum three-month commitment.

What if we already use Zapier/Make/HubSpot? Do we have to switch tools?

No. We work with whatever you already have. The goal is to make your existing tools smarter and more connected, not to rip and replace your stack. If we identify a tool that would serve you better, we'll recommend it, but the decision is always yours.

What size companies do you work with?

Primarily small and mid-market businesses, from 10-person teams to companies with a few hundred employees. We've also worked with large organizations (including Fortune 500 brand partners), but the sweet spot is companies big enough to benefit from automation and small enough to move quickly on it.

Do we own what you build?

Yes. Everything we build belongs to you. Full documentation, full access, full ownership. We don't hold your systems hostage with proprietary platforms or recurring license fees. If you want to maintain and extend it yourself after the engagement, you absolutely can.

What makes Project Baseline different from other AI consultants?

Two things. First, we actually use what we sell. This isn't theoretical knowledge from a certification course. We run production AI systems across multiple businesses every day. Second, we implement, not just advise. A lot of AI consultants hand you a strategy deck and wish you luck. We build the systems, deploy them, and make sure they work before we walk away.

Do you work with political campaigns specifically?

Yes. Todd was Interim Executive Director of the CEIDC (Committee to Elect Illinois Democratic Candidates) and has been running The Hip Hop Democrat for 18 years. We understand political operations from the inside, not just the tech side. Our campaign AI systems handle voter outreach automation, content pipelines, donor engagement, and digital strategy. We build what campaigns actually need to win, not what looks good in a demo.
