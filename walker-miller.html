Our Approach
LEARNING BY DOING, NOT BY WATCHING

This is not a lecture series about what AI can theoretically do. This is a hands-on training curriculum built around the actual tasks Walker-Miller's administrative support professionals perform every day.

Participants will learn by doing: drafting real invoice summaries, processing sample rebate documentation across multiple utility programs, generating compliant reports, and building marketing content that contractors can actually use. Every module is grounded in workflows your team already knows, with AI tools layered in as practical accelerators.

The curriculum is designed for entry-level learners and aligned with the Department of Labor's AI Literacy Framework. We build in responsible use principles from the start, so your team understands not just how to use these tools, but when to trust them, when to verify, and where human judgment stays essential.

PROMPT ENGINEERING FOR CLEAN ENERGY

75 min | Writing prompts that produce usable output
